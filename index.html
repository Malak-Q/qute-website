About QUTE: Your Quantum Adventure

Step into a futuristic lab as a Quantum Explorer! QUTE is a groundbreaking mobile game designed to demystify quantum computing. Your mission: solve intricate puzzles to fix "Quantum Instabilities" and ultimately repair a "Core Anomaly" in the lab's main processor.

We've integrated a powerful backend with Qiskit to handle complex quantum simulations, ensuring a seamless and accurate learning experience directly on your mobile device. No prior knowledge needed ‚Äì just curiosity!

Meet the Quantum Gates: Your Tools for Discovery

H

Hadamard (H)

Creates superposition: a qubit becomes both 0 and 1 at the same time!

CX

CNOT (CX)

Entangles qubits: the state of one instantly affects the other, no matter the distance.

X

Pauli-X (NOT)

Flips a qubit's state: 0 becomes 1, and 1 becomes 0. Simple, yet powerful!

Learn & Master Quantum Concepts, Step-by-Step

Structured Learning Journey

QUTE's curriculum is broken down into four clear modules. Each module builds on the last, making complex quantum ideas easy to grasp.

  • Module 1: The Quantum Basics (Qubit states, Superposition)
  • Module 2: Interacting Qubits (Multi-qubit systems, Entanglement)
  • Module 3: Simple Quantum Algorithms (Deutsch-Jozsa, Quantum Teleportation)
  • Module 4: Advanced Concepts & Algorithms (Grover's Search, QFT)
